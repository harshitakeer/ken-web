How It Works

The Modules

Each module is a mechanism designed to fit a wide range of products.

Spritz Bottle

Fine-mist spray nozzle. Perfect for cologne, perfume, body spray, facial mist, or setting spray.

Tube Applicator

Doe-foot wand applicator. Ideal for lip gloss, lip oil, liquid concealer, liquid blush, or any liquid product.

Pump

Push-down pump dispenser. Great for sunscreen, moisturizer, lotion, serum, or foundation.

Twist Stick

Retractable twist-up stick. Works for deodorant, lip balm, concealer, solid cologne, or solid perfume.

Spoolie

Wand with bristle applicator. Made for brow gel, mascara, lash serum, or hair flyaway stick.

Mini Capsule

Snap-open mini compartment. Carry breath mints, vitamins, bobby pins, earbuds, or any small essentials.
